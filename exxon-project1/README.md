## **Process Optimization & Automation in Butyl Rubber Production**

### **Overview**
During my co-op at ExxonMobil, I supported operations within the butyl rubber unit by developing process improvements, automating data workflows, and assisting with daily production monitoring. My work focused on increasing operational efficiency, reducing errors, and supporting reliable plant performance.

---

### **Problem**
The butyl rubber classification process relied on manual data handling, which introduced errors and limited scalability across sites. Additionally, inefficiencies in adsorber regeneration and recurring extrusion line plugging created operational and cost challenges.

---

### **Approach**

- Developed an automated Excel-based tool to streamline butyl rubber classification and reduce manual input errors  
- Evaluated alternative adsorber regeneration strategies using capital and operating cost comparisons  
- Monitored daily production metrics while backfilling the finishing contact engineer role  
- Collaborated with international butyl sites to troubleshoot and resolve extrusion line plugging issues  

---

### **Results**

- Reduced classification errors and enabled standardized reprocessing guidelines across multiple sites  
- Provided recommendations to improve adsorber regeneration efficiency  
- Prevented extrusion line plugging events, avoiding approximately $1,800 per incident in downtime costs  
- Reported daily production performance to support consistent plant operations  

---

### **Impact**
This work improved operational reliability and efficiency within the butyl unit while contributing to measurable cost savings and more standardized global practices.
